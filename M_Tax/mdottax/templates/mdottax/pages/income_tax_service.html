Income Tax Services

Navigating the complexities of Income taxes is crucial for financial health and compliance. At A-QuickTax, If you are a US Expat, you must file personal taxes no matter where in the world you reside. All US citizens and Green Card holders need to file an annual tax return reporting their worldwide income if they meet the minimum filing thresholds. But don't worry, most expats end up paying very little to nothing when returns are prepared correctly and with all the tax savings deductions available to them.

You have a US tax filing requirement if you have income from the US but do not meet the substantial presence test, which would require a U.S. Resident tax return, or if you were engaged in a trade or business in the United States during the calendar year; even if this generated no income, or if the income is exempt from US tax under a tax treaty or any section of the Internal Revenue Code
