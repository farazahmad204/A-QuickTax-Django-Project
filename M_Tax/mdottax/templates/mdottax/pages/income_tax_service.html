Income Tax Services

Navigating the complexities of income taxes is essential for financial health and compliance. At A-QuickTax, we specialize in helping U.S. expats, U.S. citizens, and Green Card holders with their income tax filings, regardless of where they reside. As a U.S. citizen or Green Card holder, you are required to file an annual tax return, reporting your worldwide income if you meet the minimum filing thresholds.

For many expats, tax preparation can seem daunting, but don't worry. With the right expertise, most expats end up paying little to no taxes when their returns are prepared correctly, taking advantage of all available tax savings and deductions. We ensure that all available benefits, like Foreign Earned Income Exclusion and Foreign Tax Credit, are applied to minimize your tax liability.

In addition to general expat filings, you also have a U.S. tax filing requirement if you have income from U.S. sources or were engaged in a trade or business in the United States during the calendar year, even if no income was generated, or if the income is exempt from U.S. tax under a tax treaty.
