Business Formation and ITIN

At A-QuickTax, we specialize in helping entrepreneurs, startups, and foreign nationals navigate the complexities of Business Formation and ITIN (Individual Taxpayer Identification Number) applications. Whether you're launching your business in the U.S. or need tax assistance for non-residents, we provide a seamless process to ensure you're set up for success.

Starting a business can be overwhelming, but we are here to guide you every step of the way. We offer professional business formation services, helping you choose the best legal structure and ensuring all the necessary paperwork is filed with the state. Whether you're looking to establish a Sole Proprietorship, LLC, Corporation, or a Partnership, our team will assist you in selecting the right option for your goals.

If you are a foreign national, non-resident, or someone who does not qualify for a Social Security Number (SSN), obtaining an Individual Taxpayer Identification Number (ITIN) is essential. An ITIN is required for tax reporting, filing tax returns, and engaging in various financial transactions in the U.S. Our expert team will guide you through the process, ensuring you meet all the IRS requirements.

Our Business Formation Services Include:

  • Business Structure Consultation: We help you choose the best structure—LLC, Corporation, Partnership, or Sole Proprietorship—based on your needs and goals.
  • State Registration: We handle the filing of the necessary documents, including Articles of Incorporation or Operating Agreements, with the state of your choice.
  • EIN Application: We apply for an Employer Identification Number (EIN) to set up your business for tax purposes.
  • Registered Agent Services: We act as your Registered Agent to receive official correspondence from the state.
  • Business Licenses & Permits: We help you obtain any required local, state, or federal licenses to operate legally.

Our ITIN Services Include:

  • ITIN Application Assistance: We help you complete IRS Form W-7, the required application for an ITIN.
  • Document Preparation: We assist in gathering the necessary documents (e.g., passport, visa, or tax return) to submit with your application.
  • Submission to IRS: We handle the submission of your application to the IRS and ensure timely processing.
  • Renewal Services: If you need to renew your ITIN, we can assist with the process to keep your tax records up to date.
