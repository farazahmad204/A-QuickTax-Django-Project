Business Formation and ITIN

At A-QuickTax, we specialize in helping entrepreneurs, startups, and foreign nationals navigate the complexities of business formation and obtaining an Individual Taxpayer Identification Number (ITIN) in the U.S. Whether you're launching your business or need assistance with ITIN applications for non-residents, we provide a smooth, hassle-free process to ensure you're set up for success.

Starting a business in the U.S. can be overwhelming, but we are here to guide you every step of the way. We offer expert business formation services, helping you select the best legal structure for your needs, and ensuring all the necessary paperwork is filed correctly with the state. Whether you're interested in establishing a Sole Proprietorship, LLC, Corporation, or Partnership, our experienced team will assist you in choosing the right option based on your business goals.

If you're a foreign national, non-resident, or someone who does not qualify for a Social Security Number (SSN), obtaining an Individual Taxpayer Identification Number (ITIN) is essential for tax reporting, filing tax returns, and engaging in financial transactions in the U.S. Our team of experts will guide you through the ITIN application process, ensuring compliance with all IRS requirements and making the process as straightforward as possible.

Our Business Formation Services Include:

  • Business Structure Consultation: We help you select the best structure—LLC, Corporation, Partnership, or Sole Proprietorship—based on your business model and goals.
  • State Registration: We take care of filing the necessary documents with the state, such as Articles of Incorporation or Operating Agreements, ensuring a smooth registration process.
  • EIN Application: We assist you in obtaining an Employer Identification Number (EIN) required for tax reporting and business operations.
  • Registered Agent Services: We provide registered agent services to handle official correspondence from the state on your behalf.
  • Business Licenses & Permits: We help you secure the necessary licenses and permits from local, state, and federal authorities to ensure your business is legally compliant.

Our ITIN Services Include:

  • ITIN Application Assistance: We guide you through completing IRS Form W-7, the necessary application for an ITIN.
  • Document Preparation: We help gather and prepare the required documents such as passports, visas, and tax returns to accompany your ITIN application.
  • Submission to IRS: We handle the submission of your ITIN application to the IRS and ensure it's processed without delays.
  • ITIN Renewal Services: We offer ITIN renewal assistance to ensure your tax records remain up-to-date and you stay in compliance with the IRS.
