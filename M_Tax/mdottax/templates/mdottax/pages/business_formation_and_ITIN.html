Business Formation & ITIN Services

At A-QuickTax, we specialize in helping entrepreneurs, startups, and foreign nationals navigate the complexities of business formation and obtaining an Individual Taxpayer Identification Number (ITIN) in the U.S. Our expert services are designed to streamline the process, making sure you meet all legal requirements while setting up your business or obtaining an ITIN for tax purposes.

Starting a business in the U.S. can be overwhelming, but we make it easy. We provide comprehensive business formation services, helping you choose the right legal structure—whether it's a LLC, Corporation, Partnership, or Sole Proprietorship. Our team ensures that all paperwork is filed correctly with state authorities, setting you up for success.

If you're a foreign national or someone who doesn’t qualify for a Social Security Number (SSN), obtaining an ITIN is crucial for tax reporting, filing tax returns, and engaging in financial transactions. Our team will guide you through the ITIN application process, ensuring full IRS compliance and simplifying the entire process.

Our Comprehensive Business Formation Services

  • Business Structure Consultation: We help you choose the optimal structure for your business—LLC, Corporation, Sole Proprietorship, or Partnership—based on your needs and goals.
  • State Registration: We file necessary documents like Articles of Incorporation or Operating Agreements with the state to ensure smooth registration.
  • EIN Application: We assist you in obtaining your Employer Identification Number (EIN), required for tax reporting and business operations.
  • Registered Agent Services: Our registered agent services handle official correspondence from the state on your behalf.
  • Business Licenses & Permits: We help secure necessary licenses and permits from local, state, and federal authorities to ensure full legal compliance.

Our ITIN Services

  • ITIN Application Assistance: We help you complete IRS Form W-7, the application necessary to obtain an ITIN.
  • Document Preparation: Our team gathers and prepares essential documents like passports, visas, and tax returns to accompany your ITIN application.
  • Submission to IRS: We handle the submission of your ITIN application to the IRS, ensuring timely and correct processing.
  • ITIN Renewal Services: We assist with ITIN renewals to keep your tax records updated and ensure continued IRS compliance.
